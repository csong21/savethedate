Getting to Boulder, Colorado

Flights should be in and out of Denver International Airport (DIA) which is about a 45-60 min car ride from Boulder. Fun fact: DIA is the largest airport in North America, and the second largest in the world, behind Saudi Arabiaâ€™s King Fahd International Airport. After arriving at the Denver International Airport, the easiest way to get to Boulder is by renting a car or calling an Uber. There is also a bus service that runs between the airport and Boulder downtown.
