Our Story

  • The Backstory

    Summer 1995

    We first met before we formed memories. Jamie couldn't speak, so he didn't ask Juer's number. But he gave Juer a big hug when they parted

  • First Impression

    June 18, 2007

    Fast forward twelve years, Juer hosted Jamie in Xi'an when Jamie visited China. They biked on the city wall, caught fish in the Lotus Garden. Jamie made a total mess in front of Juer's class.

  • Keeping in touch

    2013

    Six years later, Juer visited for Thanksgiving. And Jamie flew to see her in New York. We celebrated and we explored. But little did she know, Jamie was deeply in love.

  • Second Chance

    June 30, 2017

    After 10 years of pondering. Jamie finally found the courage to ask, And off into the sunset we went.
